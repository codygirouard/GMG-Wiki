Willis Library

Willis Library is located on the Library Mall in the center of the Denton Campus, contains a wide range of study spaces, 24 hour computing, and houses collections that support the Arts and Sciences. The library offers students the use of printers and laptop checkouts as well as borrowing muisc and books.
