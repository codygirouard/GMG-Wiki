McConnell Hall

McConnell Hall is home to the university's TAMS program which is a two year residential early entrance program that serves nearly 375 new high school juniors and seniors students every year. Students are admitted from every region of the state through a selective admissions process. There are 374 rooms in McConnell Hall and each of them can hold up to 2 students that are participating in the TAMS program.
