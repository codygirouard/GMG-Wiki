Coliseum

Known as the "Super Pit", the UNT Coliseum has 9,797 upholstered permanent theater type seats plus forty-eight positions for wheelchairs. With additional meeting space located on the lower level, the Coliseum is large enough to accommodate a wide range of activities and is home to the UNT basketball team and a performance stage for live concerts. In addition to basketball games, the arena routinely holds area graduations, concerts, festivals, cheer, dance and gymnastic competitions.
