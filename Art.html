Art Building

In October 2019, UNT celebrated the grand opening of its new $70 million College of Visual Arts and Design building - one of the most technologically-advanced art facilities in the world. The cutting-edge facility offers students access to and instruction in the latest digital fabrication tools, 3D scanners, 3D printers, computer numerical control routers, laser cutters and engravers, plasma cutters and waterjet cutters.
