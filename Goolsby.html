Goolsby Chapel

The Goolsby Chapel is nondenominational and welcomes everyone, plus they offer wedding services at the venue. Goolsby Chapel has a distinctive, round sanctuary replete with beautiful stained glass windows and a skylight, a high ceiling, soft natural lighting complimented by custom dimmable lighting, and comfortable seating for 64 in standard configuration.
