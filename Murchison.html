Murchison Performing Arts Center

The Murchison Performing Arts Center (MPAC) is the premiere performance facility of the prestigious University of North Texas College of Music. The award-winning facility features two world-class performance venues: The Margot and Bill Winspear Performance Hall and the Lyric Theatre, as well as the Brock Grand Lobby and several studio and rehearsal spaces.
