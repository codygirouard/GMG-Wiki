Music Building

The UNT Music Building is home to the university's College of Muisc and hosts many practice rooms inside. The music program is one of the country's largest college music programs and are globally respected for it. The college presents more than 1,100 concerts, recitals and master classes annually. UNT music alumni can be found around the world in impressive, award-winning careers across a wide-range of music professions.
