Enviornmental Science Building

The Environmental Education, Science, and Technology Building is a 117,000 square foot environmentally friendly facility providing lab, classroom, office, and exhibit facilities for UNT's Environmental Science programs, the Institute of Applied Science, and the Elm Fork Education Center.
